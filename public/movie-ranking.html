The top movies according to Danny!

These are my top 3 movies:

A Christmas Story (1983)

Who doesn't enjoy a wholesome Christmas movie? This movie was one of my favorites growing up and has become a tradition to watch in my household.

Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby (2006)

Hilarious movie that will have you rolling around on the floor. Will Ferrel is a masterclass comedian in this comedy.

Batman: The Dark Night (2008)

This action thriller will have you on the edge of your seat. Heath Ledger as "The Joker" was one of the best preformances of an actor of all time.
